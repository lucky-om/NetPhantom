✓ 100% LOCAL PRIVACY ARCHITECTURE

Local Volatile Storage & Zero Telemetry

NetPhantom is engineered to respect total local data privacy:

  • Volatile Memory Only: Captured frames and dissected protocol trees exist exclusively in volatile system RAM and are wiped upon process termination.
  • Zero Telemetry: No backend analytics, tracking pingbacks, or crash report transmitters exist in the codebase.
  • Zero External Servers: No packet logs or host IP addresses are ever transmitted to Luckyverse or third parties.
